Simple

No need to create the CAD file! Just send a description of your desired project to the 3D printer owner you connect with.

Convenient

Using 3D printers is very complicated and requires lots of time and hard work to master. You can avoid this by leveraging the expertise of existing 3D printer owners.

Affordable

Pay for each project instead of spending up to thousands of dollars on installing and maintaining a desktop 3D printer.

Benefits to Owners

Owners of 3D printers can earn side income by fulfilling order requests. More experienced owners can charge higher fees for orders that require greater skill or more material to build. Of course, less experienced owners of 3D printers can still earn money by taking on simpler projects, which can help with developing 3D printing expertise.
